Outreach

Reach owners the safe way

Start with direct mail — no DNC vendor needed. Use cold texts only if you have a federal DNC scrub service wired up.

Direct mail Recommended

Letters and Avery labels to owner mailing addresses. This is how most land shops start.

  • No DNC vendor — federal Do Not Call list does not apply to postal mail
  • No cold-call risk — owner reads on their timeline
  • Works today — export CSV or print labels from the mail queue

Cold texts Advanced

Unsolicited SMS to owner phones. High TCPA exposure without compliance tooling.

  • DNC vendor required — e.g. DNCScrub + FTC SAN before live scrub
  • Fines are real — $500–$50,000+ per violation if you skip scrub
  • Inbound leads are different — website form fills do not need this path
