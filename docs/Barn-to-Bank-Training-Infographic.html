Training modules

Four modules. Run them in order on day one.

Each module ends with a trainer tip — the thing veterans wish someone had told them.

1

Intake

Tab: Intake · ~3 min per tract
  1. Sign in at /app/login
  2. Pick county + address or APN
  3. Click Run Automation 1
  4. Toggle Internal vs Client view
  5. + Add to Pipeline
Trainer tipRefresh Website Leads every morning — inbound beats cold outreach every time.
2

Pipeline

Tab: Deals · Your command center
  1. Open the deal modal — read entitlement score
  2. Set route: Develop / Sell / Retail
  3. Check margin band vs comp lake
  4. Flag bottlenecked tracts (no sewer CCN)
  5. Print Deal Memo for partner review
Trainer tipBottlenecked deals still have value — they go to Sell, not Outreach mail.
3

Outreach

Tab: Outreach · Mail-first
  1. Stay on Direct mail tab
  2. Build Mail List → pick tracts
  3. Build Mail Queue (no DNC step)
  4. Export CSV or Print Labels
  5. Log touches in deal modal CRM
Trainer tipDo not cold-text until you have a live DNC scrub vendor. Mail works today.
4

Intel

Tab: Intel · The moat
  1. + Log Comp after every broker call
  2. Scan plat alerts near your deals
  3. Cloud Sync — start and end of day
  4. Export bundle before big meetings
  5. Audit log = your compliance paper trail
Trainer tipTexas non-disclosure means your comp lake IS the product. Feed it daily.
Decision card

How should I contact this owner?

Print this mentally before every outreach batch.

Did they contact you first (website form, callback, referral)?

YES → Respond directly

  • No DNC vendor needed
  • Call, email, or text back
  • Mark lead contacted in Website Leads
  • Run Automation 1 on their tract ASAP
OR

NO → Cold outreach

  • Start with direct mail (recommended)
  • Cold texts need federal DNC scrub vendor
  • Never skip scrub on unsolicited SMS
  • Blocked = DO NOT CONTACT
Trainer rules

What pros do vs. what rookies do

Always

  • Cloud Sync morning and night
  • Log every off-market comp you hear
  • Mail before you text
  • Read entitlement score before LOI talk
  • Use Client view when sharing externally
  • Hard-refresh after platform updates

Never

  • Cold-text without DNC scrub
  • Skip Cloud Sync and blame "the spreadsheet"
  • Outreach bottlenecked sewer-limited tracts
  • Share skip-trace phones in client memos
  • Assume Zillow comps in Texas
  • Let website leads sit uncontacted 48h+
